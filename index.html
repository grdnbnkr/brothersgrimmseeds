About Brothers Grimm Seeds

Founded by MrSoul and his partner Sly in 1996, Brothers Grimm Seeds made a triumphant return after a 12-year hiatus. The company has become widely recognized for its high-quality, stable, and effective cannabis genetics.

Brothers Grimm Seeds is known for its fast-flowering strains and impressive resin production. By selectively combining genetics according to strict principles, the company consistently offers some of the finest cannabis strains available today.

Brothers Grimm Seeds (BGS) is a celebrated company dedicated to providing lab-certified, top-notch seed stock to the cannabis cultivation community. With a strong commitment to breeding and preserving rare cannabis genetics, BGS has earned a reputation for delivering outstanding seed varieties with exceptional characteristics. Their seeds undergo stringent testing at a Colorado state-approved lab, ensuring purity, the absence of noxious elements, and protection from harmful viruses and fungi.

As a proud partner of Brothers Grimm Seeds, we warmly welcome you to embark on a reliable, worry-free cultivation journey with their clean seed stock.
